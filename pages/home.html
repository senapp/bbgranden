B&B Gränden



Hotel B&B Gränden is located in the center of Skellefteå, only 75 meters from the Bus Station. All guests have free access to a fitness center and a shared lounge with billiards.

We have 22 rooms with space for about 60 guests. We have affordable rooms with shared shower and toilet and access to a large shared kitchen. We also have newly renovated apartments / hotel rooms in different sizes with shower and toilet in the room. Our largest apartment is 90 sqm with space for 6 people in three separate bedrooms. All apartments have their own kitchen.

A continental breakfast is served every morning at the hotel.

Inside the courtyard there is free parking for all guests. There is free Wifi for all hotel guests

Skellefteå Airport is 15 km away, and the airport bus stops 400 meters from the hotel.
