B&B Gränden



Set within 500 metres of Västerbotten Theatre in Skellefteå, B&B Gränden has a fitness centre and a shared lounge, as well as free WiFi throughout the property.


The units at the bed and breakfast are equipped with a seating area.


Selected rooms will provide you with a kitchen with a microwave.


A continental breakfast is served every morning at the property.


You can play billiards at B&B Gränden.


Skelleftea Airport is 15 km from the property.
